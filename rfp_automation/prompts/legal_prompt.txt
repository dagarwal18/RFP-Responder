## Your Task
Perform a comprehensive legal review AND generate a substantive legal section for the proposal. Produce a JSON object with the following keys.
Return JSON only. No markdown, code fences, or commentary.

### 1. clause_risks
For each contract clause identified:
- **clause_id**: Unique identifier (e.g., "CLAUSE-001")
- **clause_text**: The clause text (abbreviated if long)
- **risk_level**: "LOW", "MEDIUM", "HIGH", or "CRITICAL"
- **concern**: What specific risk does this clause pose? Be concrete and clause-specific, not generic.
- **recommendation**: "accept", "negotiate", or "reject"

Consider the pre-computed rule scores. If a clause was auto-blocked, it MUST be CRITICAL.
Compare each clause against our standard terms. Flag deviations, especially:
- Liability caps that exceed 2x contract value
- IP transfer broader than work product
- Non-compete longer than the contract term
- Indemnification without mutual obligations
- Missing data protection provisions
- When recommending negotiation, state the specific fallback position we should propose.

### 2. risk_register_summary
2-3 paragraphs summarizing: overall risk posture, key negotiation points, recommended next steps.

### 3. legal_narrative
A 400-600 word professional legal section for inclusion in the proposal. This section should:
- Summarize how our company's standard terms and conditions address the RFP's legal requirements
- Reference specific company terms from the Knowledge Base (e.g., "Our standard Master Services Agreement includes...")
- Address data protection, confidentiality, liability, and IP provisions explicitly
- Note any deviations from the RFP's requested terms that require negotiation
- Include a short, clause-specific exceptions register inside the narrative when deviations exist
- Be written in formal proposal language suitable for client presentation
- If the KB does not contain relevant legal terms, state that explicitly — do NOT fabricate terms

IMPORTANT: The legal_narrative must be evidence-based. Every claim about our company's legal position MUST reference actual KB data provided above. If the KB lacks information on a topic, say "Details to be provided upon contract negotiation" rather than inventing terms.

### 4. decision
- **APPROVED**: No HIGH or CRITICAL risks. All clauses acceptable.
- **CONDITIONAL**: HIGH risks present but negotiable.
- **BLOCKED**: Any CRITICAL risk, unlimited liability, missing required certifications.

### 5. block_reasons
List of specific reasons if BLOCKED, otherwise empty list.

### 6. confidence
Float from 0.0 to 1.0.

CRITICAL RULES:
- Do NOT override auto-block decisions from rule scores.
- Be conservative: when in doubt, flag as higher risk.
- Focus on substantive legal risks, not formatting.
- NEVER fabricate or assume legal terms not in the KB data.

Return valid JSON: clause_risks, risk_register_summary, legal_narrative, decision, block_reasons, confidence
